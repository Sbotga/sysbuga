SYSbuga requires message content intent to provide fast and easy minigames.

Our "guessing" minigames, one of the main features of our bot, use the prefix `-...` to guess the answer to a cropped or otherwise modified image.

A user may guess multiple times, and this is a collaborative game, so users will publicly guess and share answers to see what's right and what's wrong. However, they are on a time limit (depending on the mode).

Using the prefix `-` for guesses is the most convenient and fastest way to allow users to guess multiple answers as fast as they can think of them.
